Cannon Shot by Akshat Tandon 201503001

Game Objective:
Shoot all the targets to win the game. You have only 10 bombs to shoot them.
If you are able to successfully shoot all the targets in <=10 shots you win the game.
If you have used all the bombs and some targets are still remaining you loose.

Controls:
Spacebar to shoot the projectile from cannon
A/B to tilt the cannon
F/S to alter projectile speed
LEFT,/RIGHT to pan the scene
UP/DOWN to zoom
